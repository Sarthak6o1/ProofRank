# Senior AI Engineer — Founding Team

**Company:** Redrob AI (Series A AI-native talent intelligence platform)  
**Location:** Pune/Noida, India (Hybrid)  
**Experience:** 5–9 years (soft band)

## Role mandate

Own the intelligence layer of Redrob's product: ranking, retrieval, and matching systems for recruiters and candidates.

## Must-have signals

- Production embeddings-based retrieval (sentence-transformers, BGE, E5, OpenAI embeddings, etc.)
- Vector DB / hybrid search (FAISS, Pinecone, Elasticsearch, Milvus, etc.)
- Strong Python in production
- Ranking evaluation frameworks (NDCG, MRR, MAP, offline-to-online correlation, A/B tests)

## Nice-to-have

- LLM fine-tuning (LoRA, QLoRA)
- Learning-to-rank
- HR-tech / marketplace product experience

## Explicit disqualifiers

- Title-chasers (many short tenures)
- LangChain-only recent AI experience without prior production ML/IR
- Consulting-only careers (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, etc.) with no product-company history
- CV/speech/robotics primary without NLP/IR exposure
- Keyword-stuffed skill lists without career proof

## Ideal profile

- 6–8 years total, 4–5 in applied ML at product companies
- Shipped end-to-end search/ranking/rec system at scale
- India-based or willing to relocate to Pune/Noida
- Active on platform / genuinely available to hire

## Hackathon note

The right answer is **not** "most AI keywords in skills." Rank by career proof, JD intent, and behavioral availability. Tier-5 plain-language fits (recommendation systems at product companies without buzzwords) are valid. Keyword stuffers and honeypots are traps.
